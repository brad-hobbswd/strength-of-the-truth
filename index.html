A Story About Pain Nobody Saw

David stood in the dim light of his bathroom, staring at a reflection that no longer felt like his own. His jaw was tight, his shoulders tense, and the faint bruise along his ribs reminded him of the accident at work he had tried to ignore. Pain radiated through his side with every breath, but he told himself the same thing he always did.

"You're fine. You can handle it."

Outside, the world was waking up. Inside, he felt like he was sinking.
